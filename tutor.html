UMSI Tutoring & Academic Support

UMSI Programming Peer Tutoring

UMSI Peer Tutors provide computational tutoring assistance for students enrolled in SI 106, SI 206, SI 506, and SI 507; or for students who need general Python support. The tutoring team is composed of both graduate and undergraduate students. Each tutor maintains their own appointment calendar and one-on-one and group tutoring sessions are offered free of charge. If you require additional programming guidance and support this semester consider contacting a Peer Tutor.

UMSI Programming Peer Tutoring Information Available for the below courses:

  • SI 106 Programs, Information, and People
  • SI 206 Data-Oriented Programming
  • SI 506 Programming I

English Language Institute

ELI exclusively serves members of the University of Michigan community with English for Academic Purposes courses and resources, GSI preparation, and TESOL courses that prepare students to teach English as a second or foreign language.
